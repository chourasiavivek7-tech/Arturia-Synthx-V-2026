## 📖 About

**Arturia Synthx V 2026** is a faithful recreation of the iconic Synthex hardware synthesizer, now available as a standalone software instrument for Windows and macOS. This 2026 edition brings the legendary lush pads, rich strings, and iconic punch of the original into your DAW, with modern enhancements like an expanded modulation matrix, integrated effects, and a streamlined preset browser. Whether you're a producer, sound designer, or synth enthusiast, Synthx V delivers the classic character of a rare vintage machine with the convenience of modern plugin technology.

## ⚙️ Requirements

- **Operating System:** Windows 10 (64-bit) or later / macOS 10.15 or later
- **Processor:** Intel Core i5 or AMD equivalent (Apple Silicon supported natively on Mac)
- **RAM:** 4 GB minimum (8 GB recommended)
- **Storage:** 2 GB  disk space
- **Internet Connection:** Required for initial activation and preset 
- **DAW:** Any VST3, AU, or AAX-compatible host (e.g., Ableton Live, FL Studio, Logic Pro, Cubase)
- **Display:** 1280x800 or higher resolution

## ✨ Features

- **Authentic Engine** 🎚️ — Carefully modeled analog circuitry captures the original's warm, rich tone and unique filter character.
- **Expanded Modulation** 🔗 — 4 envelopes, 2 LFOs, and a flexible modulation matrix for deep sound design.
- **Built-In Effects Suite** 🌊 — Includes stereo chorus, phaser, delay, and reverb, all optimized for the synth's signal path.
- **Enhanced Preset Library** 📂 — Over 400 factory presets spanning vintage classics to modern productions, easily browsable with tags.
- **Advanced Unison** 🎹 — Up to 8 voices of unison with detune and spread for massive, evolving pads.
- **Scalable Interface** 🖥️ — Resizable GUI with Retina/HiDPI support for crisp visuals at any size.
- **MIDI Learn & Automation** 🎛️ — Map any parameter to your controller or automate directly in your DAW.
- **Mono/Poly/Arpeggiator** 🔄 — Flexible voice modes with an integrated 16-step arpeggiator and chord memory.
